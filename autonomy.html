A.V.C.COLLEGE (AUTONOMOUS)

A.V.C.College was conferred autonomy in the year 1987 by the University Grants Commission and was Reaccredited and awarded "A" Grants status with CGPA of 3.3/4.00 by the National Assessment and Accrediation Council in their third Cycle in 2003.Under autonomy,the college has the freedom to determine and prescribe it's own courses of studies and syllabi, prescribed rules of admission (subject to the reservation policy of the State Government),evovle methods of evaluation and to conduct examintions.The degrees are awarded by the Bharathidasan University,Thiruchirappalli under it's seal with the name of the college mentioned.
