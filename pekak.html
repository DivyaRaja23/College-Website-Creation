PEKAK

'PEKAK' is the name given to our college Magazine.Each letterr in the word 'PEKAK' stands for a villages of the Anbanathapuram Vahaira.

The five villages,
P for Pallavarayanpet
E for Enathimangalam
K for Kanganamputhur
A for Anbanathapuram
K for Karugudi

constitute the Anbanathapuram Vahaira,reputed for munificence from the hoary past.As a token of our gratitude to the five philanthropic families,we have named our Magazine "PEKAK".
