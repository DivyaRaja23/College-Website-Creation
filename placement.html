OBJECTIVE

Placement and entrepreneurial development cell is functioning in the college sinceautonomy and offers service to students. Many placement opportunities are created for the posts of Chemists, Software Persons, Accounts Staff, Teachers, etc., in many Companies/Schools. Workshops on Personality development, interview skills, etc., are conducted periodically.

FUTURE PLAN OF THE CELL
  • The Cell will contact more number of employers and to create enough placement opportunities to all students
  • The Cell will maintain a close relationship with AVCC Alumni to arrange for more placements to our students.
  • The Cell will conduct more training programmes/workshops to train all final year students on soft-skills, especially Communication skills in English and problem solving techniques in Mathematical aptitude.
  • The Cell will provide intensive training to use Laptop sponsored by the state government in career avenues & employment opportunities.
  • The Cell is trying to get 100% placement for the aspiring students.
